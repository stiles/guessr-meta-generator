Rwanda

Language

Kinyarwanda (official), French, English; uses Latin alphabet

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are typically white with a blue band at the top featuring the Rwandan flag.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are often in Kinyarwanda, with some bilingual signs in French or English; many signs are well-maintained and use bright colors.

Environment

Rwanda is known as the 'Land of a Thousand Hills,' featuring lush green landscapes, rolling hills, and volcanic mountains.

Infrastructure

Urban areas have modern buildings with some colonial architecture; rural areas may feature traditional mud houses with thatched roofs.

Culture

Look for traditional markets, colorful fabrics, and public murals depicting Rwandan culture; the presence of gorilla trekking signs in certain areas.

Bonus

Rwanda has a clean and organized appearance in urban areas; the presence of street vendors and gatherings in town centers can be a clue.
