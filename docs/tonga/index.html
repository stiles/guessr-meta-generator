Tonga

Language

Tongan is the official language, using the Latin alphabet without any special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates in Tonga typically have a blue background with white letters and numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in English and Tongan, with simple designs and often colorful backgrounds.

Environment

Tonga consists of lush tropical islands, sandy beaches, coral reefs, and volcanic landscapes.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles include traditional Tongan fale (thatched roof houses) and colonial-style buildings.

Culture

Distinctive cultural markers include Tongan fale, traditional tapa cloth, and signs of Polynesian heritage.

Bonus

Tonga has a warm tropical climate, and the presence of palm trees and local flora can be indicators.
