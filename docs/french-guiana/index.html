French Guiana

Language

French is the official language. The alphabet is the same as in standard French, with no additional special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue strip on the left side that has the French flag and the region code '973'.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are similar to those in mainland France, featuring clear French text and standard symbols.

Environment

Lush tropical rainforest, riverine landscapes, and coastal areas with mangroves and beaches.

Infrastructure

Buildings reflect a mix of French colonial architecture and modern styles, with some indigenous influences.

Culture

Presence of diverse cultural influences including Creole, indigenous, and Hmong communities, with local markets and cuisine.

Bonus

Look for tropical flora, signs in French, and occasional indigenous art or architecture.
