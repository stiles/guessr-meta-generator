Denmark

Language

Danish is the official language, using the Latin alphabet with special characters æ, ø, and å.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top, featuring a combination of letters and numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally blue and white or green and white, with clear, easy-to-read fonts. They often include pictograms.

Environment

Denmark features flat landscapes, coastal areas, and farmland, with a mix of urban and rural settings.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles vary from traditional half-timbered houses to modern Scandinavian designs, often characterized by minimalism and functionality.

Culture

Look for bicycles as a common mode of transport, and the presence of Danish flags (Dannebrog) in homes and public spaces.

Bonus

Street names often include 'gade' (street) or 'vej' (way). The presence of wind turbines is also common in many areas.
