Maldives

Language

Dhivehi is the official language, written in the Thaana script which includes unique characters like ހ, ދ, and މ.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue border. They often have a unique number system.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs often feature English text, with some signs in Dhivehi. Signs are typically colorful and include pictograms.

Environment

The Maldives consists of flat, low-lying islands with white sandy beaches, turquoise lagoons, and coral reefs.

Infrastructure

Architecture typically features wooden structures and thatched roofs, with modern buildings in urban areas like Malé.

Culture

Distinctive markers include Islamic architecture, traditional Dhoni boats, and vibrant local markets.

Bonus

Look for palm trees, clear blue waters, and resorts often depicted in the imagery.
