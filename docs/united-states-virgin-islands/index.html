United States Virgin Islands

Language

English is the official language; Virgin Islands Creole is also spoken.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue border and display 'U.S. Virgin Islands' along with a unique number.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in English and may feature tropical motifs; signs are often reflective.

Environment

The landscape includes tropical vegetation, mountainous areas, and coastal beaches; the climate is tropical.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles include Caribbean colonial and modern constructions with vibrant colors; urban areas may have a laid-back, beach town feel.

Culture

Cultural markers include local festivals, Caribbean music, and cuisine featuring seafood and tropical fruits.

Bonus

Look for distinctive palm trees, coastal views, and the presence of local markets and street vendors.
