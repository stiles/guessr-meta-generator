Pitcairn Islands

Language

English, with some influence from Polynesian languages; standard Latin alphabet

Cars

Vehicles may have British-style registration plates, but there are very few cars due to the small population

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road

Signs

Road signs are generally simple, often in English, with limited variety due to low traffic

Environment

Rugged terrain with steep cliffs, lush greenery, and a coastal environment; volcanic origin

Infrastructure

Limited urban infrastructure; buildings often made of wood, with a colonial architectural influence; few streets

Culture

Islander culture with a strong sense of community; unique local traditions and historical sites

Bonus

Very few people, remote location, and distinctive flora; possible views of the settlement of Adamstown
