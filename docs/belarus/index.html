Belarus

Home > Europe > Eastern Europe > Belarus

Language

Belarusian and Russian; uses Cyrillic alphabet.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with blue bands at the top; the code for Belarus is 'BY'.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs typically use Cyrillic script and may also feature symbols and colors that are distinctively European.

Environment

Flat terrain with extensive forests, rivers, and lakes; includes agricultural fields.

Infrastructure

Mix of Soviet-era architecture and modern buildings; often includes large public squares and wide boulevards.

Culture

Traditional wooden houses can be found in rural areas; Belarusian motifs may be present in local art and crafts.

Bonus

Presence of rural landscapes and villages; often see public transport buses; distinctive Soviet-era monuments.
