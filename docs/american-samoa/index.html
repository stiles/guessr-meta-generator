American Samoa

Language

Samoan and English; the alphabet is the Latin alphabet with some additional letters like Ê» (okina) for glottal stops.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates typically display a white background with blue text, featuring the abbreviation 'AS' for American Samoa.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in English and Samoan, with a mix of standard American road sign styles and local variations.

Environment

The landscape features lush tropical vegetation, volcanic mountains, and coastal areas with coral reefs.

Infrastructure

Buildings often exhibit traditional Samoan architecture, such as fale (open-sided structures), along with some modern concrete structures.

Culture

Cultural markers include traditional Samoan tattoos, local art, fale, and public gatherings that reflect Samoan customs and community life.

Bonus

Google Street View may show vibrant local markets, traditional festivals, and distinct natural vistas such as the National Park of American Samoa.
