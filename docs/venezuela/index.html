Venezuela

Language

Spanish; uses the Latin alphabet with special characters such as ñ and accented vowels (á, é, í, ó, ú)

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top that features the Venezuelan coat of arms, and they often start with a letter followed by numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in Spanish, with a mix of standard international symbols and unique local variations; signs often feature bright colors.

Environment

Diverse geographical features including the Andes mountains, Amazon rainforest, and Caribbean coastline; tropical climate in many areas.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may display a mix of colonial architecture and modern buildings, with notable structures like the Parque Central Complex in Caracas.

Culture

Venezuelan culture is marked by colorful murals, street art, and local music styles; look for signs of traditional celebrations or local markets.

Bonus

Look for distinctive palm trees and tropical vegetation; rural areas often feature dirt roads and small houses with vibrant colors.
