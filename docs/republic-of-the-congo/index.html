Republic of the Congo

Language

French is the official language, with a Latin alphabet. Local languages include Lingala, Kituba, and others.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top displaying the name 'Congo'. The format often includes a combination of letters and numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in French, featuring standard European styles. They may have additional local signage.

Environment

The landscape includes dense rainforests, savannas, and rivers, particularly the Congo River. Urban areas may have a mix of green spaces.

Infrastructure

Urban areas feature a mix of colonial and modern architecture, with buildings often made of concrete. Roads may be unpaved in rural areas.

Culture

Look for vibrant street markets, traditional music and dance, and local art. People may be seen wearing colorful fabrics.

Bonus

Street View may show significant landmarks like the Basilique Saint-Anne, traditional huts, or local wildlife. Look for palm trees and tropical vegetation.
