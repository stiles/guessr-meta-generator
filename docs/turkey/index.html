Turkey

Language

Turkish; uses the Latin alphabet with special characters such as ç, ğ, ö, ş, ü.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left containing the province code and the Turkish flag.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in Turkish and use a mix of standard road sign shapes and colors; highway signs often have a blue background.

Environment

Diverse landscapes including mountains, beaches, and forests; Mediterranean climate in coastal areas, with more continental climates inland.

Infrastructure

Common architectural styles include Ottoman, modernist, and a mix of historical buildings; urban areas often feature high-rise buildings and wide boulevards.

Culture

Distinctive markers include mosques with minarets, bazaars, and traditional Turkish tea houses.

Bonus

Street View might show unique local markets, traditional Turkish carpets, and the presence of cats in urban environments.
