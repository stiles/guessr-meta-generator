Clues

Language

Arabic (official), Kurdish; Arabic script with Latin script in some areas

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a colored band at the top, usually blue or red, displaying the governorate name and vehicle type.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right-hand side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in Arabic, with some bilingual signs in Kurdish in northern regions; signs often feature a mix of Latin and Arabic numerals.

Environment

Iraq features a mix of desert landscapes, river valleys (especially around the Tigris and Euphrates), and mountainous areas in the north.

Infrastructure

Architecture ranges from modern urban buildings to traditional mud-brick structures, with notable historical sites and mosques featuring domes and minarets.

Culture

Look for traditional markets (souks), local food stalls, and cultural symbols such as the Iraqi flag or references to Mesopotamian history.
