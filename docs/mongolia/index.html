Clues

Use these details to boost your ability to identify specific countries in the game.

Language

Mongolian is the official language, written in Cyrillic script, which includes characters such as Ө, Ү, and Җ.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left side. The first letter indicates the region.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in Mongolian Cyrillic, with standard colors like blue for informational signs and red for prohibitory signs.

Environment

The landscape is characterized by vast steppes, mountains, and deserts, with a generally arid climate.

Infrastructure

Urban areas feature a mix of Soviet-era architecture and modern constructions, with low-rise buildings common in rural areas.

Culture

Look for traditional ger (yurt) structures in rural settings, as well as nomadic herding practices.

Bonus

Google Street View may show wide open roads with little traffic, often surrounded by nature and few urban developments.
