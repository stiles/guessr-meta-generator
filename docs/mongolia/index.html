Language

Mongolian is the official language, using the Cyrillic alphabet with some unique characters such as 'ү', 'ө', and 'ҳ'.

Look for traditional ger (yurt) structures in rural settings, as well as nomadic herding practices.

Miscellaneous

Environment

The landscape is characterized by vast steppes, mountains, and deserts, with a generally arid climate.

Topography

Mongolia is characterized by vast steppes, mountain ranges such as the Altai and Khangai, and deserts like the Gobi, making it predominantly mountainous and flat.
