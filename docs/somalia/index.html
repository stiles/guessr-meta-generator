Somalia

Language

Somali and Arabic; Somali uses the Latin alphabet with some special characters like 'q' and 'x'.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are usually white with a blue band at the top and include Somali scripts and numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs may be in Somali and Arabic; they often have a simple design and can include both languages.

Environment

The landscape features arid terrains, coastal areas, and some mountainous regions, particularly in the north.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may show a mix of modern and traditional architectural styles, with some concrete buildings and colorful markets.

Culture

Look for signs of nomadic culture, traditional clothing like the 'dirac' for women, and the presence of camels or goats.

Bonus

Street View may show local markets, mosques, and evidence of the Somali civil architecture.
