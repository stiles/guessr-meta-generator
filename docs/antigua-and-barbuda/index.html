Antigua and Barbuda

Language

English is the official language, with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue border, featuring a combination of letters and numbers. The plates include the name 'Antigua and Barbuda'.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs often use English and are generally white with black text. Warning signs are typically triangular, and regulatory signs are rectangular.

Environment

The landscape features a mix of beautiful beaches, tropical forests, and hilly terrain. The islands are surrounded by clear blue waters.

Infrastructure

Architecture includes colonial-style buildings, brightly colored homes, and modern hotels. Urban areas may have a mix of concrete and traditional wooden structures.

Culture

Look for signs of Caribbean culture such as local markets, festivals, and music events. The presence of cricket fields is also common.

Bonus

Check for palm trees, sandy beaches, and local wildlife. The distinct colorful houses and small, intimate community settings are also indicators.
