Angola

Language

Portuguese is the official language. The alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are typically white with a blue band on the left side, displaying the province code.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally in Portuguese, with a mix of standard European and local symbols.

Environment

Angola features diverse landscapes, including coastal areas, savannas, and mountainous regions, with a generally warm climate.

Infrastructure

Urban areas display a mix of colonial Portuguese architecture and modern buildings, often with colorful facades.

Culture

Cultural markers include traditional Angolan crafts, music styles like Semba, and local markets.

Bonus

Look for signs of Portuguese influence in architecture and the presence of palm trees in the landscape.
