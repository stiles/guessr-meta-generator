Tokelau

Language

Tokelauan is the official language, using the Latin alphabet without any special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are usually white with a black font, often featuring the word 'Tokelau' and the designation 'NZ' for New Zealand.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in Tokelauan and English, often featuring simple designs with minimal text.

Environment

The landscape consists of low-lying islands with sandy beaches, palm trees, and coral reefs, surrounded by the Pacific Ocean.

Infrastructure

Buildings are usually simple and modest, often made of wood or other local materials, with a focus on functionality rather than modern architectural styles.

Culture

Distinctive markers include traditional Polynesian motifs in art and decor, and the presence of community gathering places.

Bonus

Look for the absence of large urban developments, prevalence of natural scenery, and small-scale local shops or community centers.
