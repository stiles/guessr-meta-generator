Clues

Use these details to boost your ability to identify specific countries in the game.

Language

Russian is the official language, using the Cyrillic alphabet. Special characters include letters like ё, ж, ч, ш, and ы.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left side that displays the region code. The format is typically 1234 AB 78.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs often have a mix of Cyrillic text and international symbols. Warning signs are usually triangular, and informational signs are rectangular.

Environment

Russia features diverse landscapes including vast steppes, dense forests, mountains (like the Ural Mountains), and extensive rivers (such as the Volga).

Infrastructure

Architectural styles range from Soviet-era brutalism to modern skyscrapers in cities. Traditional wooden dachas can also be found in rural areas.

Culture

Cultural markers may include Orthodox churches with onion domes, matryoshka dolls, and public spaces with statues of historical figures.

Bonus

Look for distinctive street furniture like bus stop designs and unique public transport vehicles, as well as Cyrillic text on storefronts.
