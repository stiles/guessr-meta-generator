Language

Arabic is the official language, using the Arabic alphabet which includes special characters such as ع, غ, ق, and ط.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates typically have a white background with a blue strip on the left containing the Palestinian flag. The plates have Arabic and Latin characters.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in Arabic, often with Latin script as well. Signs may include specific symbols and use vibrant colors.

Environment

The landscape features a mix of urban areas, olive groves, hilly terrains, and some desert-like regions.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles vary from traditional stone houses to modern buildings, with many structures reflecting Middle Eastern influences.

Culture

Look for signs of Palestinian culture, such as traditional dress, markets, and symbols like the olive tree.
