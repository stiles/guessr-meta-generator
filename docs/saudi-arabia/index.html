Arabic is the official language, using the Arabic script. Other languages include English and various regional dialects.

The landscape includes vast deserts, mountainous regions, and occasional oases; the terrain is generally arid with sandy or rocky surfaces.

Saudi Arabia features vast desert landscapes, with mountain ranges in the west (e.g., the Sarawat Mountains) and flat areas like the Arabian Peninsula's central plateau.
