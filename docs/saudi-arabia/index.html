Saudi Arabia

Language

Arabic is the official language, written in the Arabic script which features cursive writing and dots above or below letters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates typically have Arabic numerals and letters, often featuring the name of the province and the registration year.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are predominantly in Arabic with some bilingual signs in English; they often use clear, bold fonts and are usually reflective.

Environment

The landscape includes vast deserts, mountainous regions, and occasional oases; the terrain is generally arid with sandy or rocky surfaces.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles vary from traditional mud-brick houses to modern skyscrapers; urban areas may feature Islamic architecture with domes and arches.

Culture

Distinctive features include mosques with minarets, traditional markets (souks), and the presence of Islamic symbols and dress codes.

Bonus

Look for palm trees, camel imagery, and Arabic calligraphy in public spaces.
