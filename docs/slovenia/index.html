Slovenia

Language

Slovenian is the official language, using the Latin alphabet with special characters such as č, ž, and š.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with blue lettering, featuring the 'SLO' country code on the left side.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally in Slovenian, with standard European shapes and colors. Many signs are blue for highways and green for local roads.

Environment

Slovenia features diverse landscapes, including the Julian Alps, rolling hills, dense forests, and the Adriatic coastline.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles include a mix of Baroque, Gothic, and modern designs, with notable influences from Austro-Hungarian and Venetian styles.

Culture

Distinctive markers include traditional wooden houses, cultural festivals, and vineyards in the countryside.

Bonus

Look for unique features such as the Triglav symbol, which can appear in various locations, and the characteristic green color of Slovenia's nature.
