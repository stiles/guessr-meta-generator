Language

The official languages are Dari (Persian) and Pashto. The alphabet used is the Arabic script, with additional letters for specific sounds.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are typically white with black lettering. Plates often have a blue strip on the left side indicating the province.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in Dari and Pashto, often using the Arabic script. Signs may also include pictograms and may not always follow standard international road sign conventions.

Environment

Afghanistan features a diverse landscape including mountainous regions (like the Hindu Kush), deserts, and valleys. The environment varies significantly across the country.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may showcase a mix of traditional Afghan architecture and modern buildings. Common elements include mud-brick houses, mosques with domes, and bazaars.

Culture

Cultural markers include traditional clothing such as the shalwar kameez, bazaars with local crafts, and significant historical sites like the Buddhas of Bamiyan.

Bonus
