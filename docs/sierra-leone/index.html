Sierra Leone

Language

English is the official language, with Krio widely spoken. The alphabet is Latin-based, with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue strip at the top; they typically include a combination of letters and numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally in English, with some signs featuring pictograms. Stop signs and speed limit signs are common.

Environment

Sierra Leone features a mix of coastal areas, forests, and mountainous regions. The landscape is often lush and green, especially during the rainy season.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may have a mix of colonial and modern architecture, with concrete buildings and some traditional wooden structures. Streets can be narrow and less developed in rural areas.

Culture

Look for local markets, vibrant street life, and cultural festivals. Traditional clothing may be observed, especially in rural areas.

Bonus

The presence of palm trees, local street vendors selling food, and the general tropical ambiance are distinctive.
