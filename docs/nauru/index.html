Nauru

Language

Nauruan is the official language. English is also widely spoken. The alphabet used is the Latin alphabet, with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates in Nauru are typically white with black letters and numbers. The plates often feature the word 'Nauru' at the top.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs in Nauru are often in English and use standard road sign shapes and colors. They may include distances in kilometers.

Environment

Nauru is a small island country with a flat landscape, surrounded by coral reefs. The interior features phosphate deposits, and there are some tropical plants along the coast.

Infrastructure

Buildings in Nauru tend to be low-rise and utilitarian, with some colonial-style architecture. The main town, Yaren, has basic urban infrastructure.

Culture

Cultural markers include traditional Nauruan dances, music, and the use of local motifs in art. There are also signs of the island's phosphate mining history.

Bonus

Unique features may include the absence of large urban areas, the presence of palm trees, and the distinctive appearance of the island's phosphate mines.
