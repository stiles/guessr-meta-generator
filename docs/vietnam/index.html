Presence of street food stalls, traditional markets, and the conical hat (nón lá); unique cultural icons like the lotus flower.

Diverse landscapes, including rice paddies, mountains, and coastal areas; tropical climate in the south and temperate conditions in the north.

A mix of modern high-rises and traditional architecture, including colonial-era buildings; often visible street vendors and motorbike traffic.
