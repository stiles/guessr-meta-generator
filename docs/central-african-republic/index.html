Central African Republic

Language

French is the official language; Sango is widely spoken. The alphabet uses the Latin script.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates typically start with 'CA' for Central African Republic. They are white with blue text.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are often in French, with a mix of standard European road sign shapes and some unique local variations.

Environment

The landscape features savannahs, rainforests, and rivers. The terrain is generally flat with some hilly areas.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may have colonial-style buildings, while rural areas consist of simple structures made from local materials.

Culture

Look for traditional huts, markets with local crafts, and signs of vibrant local festivals and music.

Bonus

Google Street View may show wildlife and rural road conditions, including dirt roads and unpaved paths.
