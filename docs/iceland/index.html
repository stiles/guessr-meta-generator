Iceland

Home > Europe > Northern Europe > Iceland

Language

Icelandic, which uses the Latin alphabet with special characters such as Þ (thorn), ð (eth), and æ (ash).

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue stripe on the left side, featuring the national flag and the country code 'IS'.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in Icelandic, with unique symbols and often feature a blue background for informational signs and yellow for warning signs.

Environment

The landscape includes volcanic terrains, glaciers, geysers, hot springs, and a rugged coastline. Iceland is known for its dramatic scenery and unique geological features.

Infrastructure

Urban areas feature a mix of modern and traditional architecture, often with colorful houses and turf-roofed buildings, particularly in older settlements.

Culture

Distinctive markers include traditional Icelandic houses, outdoor geothermal pools, and the presence of the Icelandic horse. Cultural references to Norse mythology may also be visible.

Bonus

Look for signs of geothermal energy usage, such as steam rising from the ground, and unique natural formations like basalt columns or lava fields.
