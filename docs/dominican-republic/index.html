Dominican Republic

Language

Spanish; uses the Latin alphabet with standard characters

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with blue lettering; plates often feature the country's coat of arms

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road

Signs

Road signs are usually in Spanish, with common use of vibrant colors and unique shapes; highway signs often have a blue background

Environment

Diverse landscapes including beaches, mountains, and tropical forests; often features palm trees and coastal scenery

Infrastructure

Colonial architecture is common in older cities like Santo Domingo; modern buildings in urban areas; street layout can be irregular

Culture

Presence of merengue and bachata music visible through street performances; colorful murals and local markets; signs of Spanish colonial influence

Bonus

Tropical vegetation, frequent presence of motorbikes, and a mix of urban and rural settings; often see people selling goods on the roadside
