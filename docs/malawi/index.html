Malawi

Language

English is the official language, with Chichewa widely spoken. The alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue stripe at the top, and the format typically includes letters followed by numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally in English, with some using Chichewa; signs may feature a mix of pictograms and text.

Environment

Malawi features diverse landscapes, including lakes (notably Lake Malawi), mountains, and rolling hills. The vegetation is typically tropical.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may show a mix of colonial-era buildings and modern constructions, with some areas having thatched-roof houses in rural settings.

Culture

Look for traditional markets, local crafts, and cultural expressions like music and dance visible in public spaces.

Bonus

Street View may show scenes of vibrant street life, roadside vendors, and unique local transport such as bicycles and minibuses.
