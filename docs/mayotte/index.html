Mayotte

Language

French is the official language. The alphabet is Latin-based with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are similar to those in France, usually white with blue bands on the left side indicating the region.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally in French and follow European standards, with a mix of local language and symbols.

Environment

Mayotte features a tropical climate with lush vegetation, volcanic landscapes, and coral reefs. The coastline is dotted with beaches.

Infrastructure

Architectural styles include a mix of traditional wooden houses and modern structures, with many buildings influenced by French colonial design.

Culture

Distinctive markers include traditional markets, local music, and a blend of African and French influences in customs and attire.

Bonus

Look for the presence of mangroves, traditional dhow boats, and the unique blend of cultures in street scenes.
