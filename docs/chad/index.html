Chad

Language

French is the official language. Arabic is also widely spoken. The alphabet is Latin with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band at the top. The format usually includes a combination of letters and numbers.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are often in French and may include both French and Arabic. Signs can be less standardized and may vary in quality.

Environment

The landscape features a mix of desert, savanna, and mountainous regions. Sand dunes and acacia trees are common in many areas.

Infrastructure

Urban areas may feature a mix of modern and traditional architecture, with many buildings made of mud brick. Paved roads are typically found in larger cities.

Culture

Cultural markers include traditional markets, local crafts, and a mix of ethnic groups such as the Sara, Arab, and Kanembou.

Bonus

Dusty roads, occasional livestock on roads, and the presence of traditional huts or homes can be notable. Look for people wearing traditional attire.
