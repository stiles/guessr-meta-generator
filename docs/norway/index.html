Norway

Language

Norwegian (Bokmål and Nynorsk), uses the Latin alphabet with special characters (æ, ø, å)

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left; they feature a combination of letters and numbers, with the first letters indicating the county.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically blue and white for information signs, with distinctive shapes; warning signs are often yellow.

Environment

Diverse landscapes including fjords, mountains, forests, and coastal areas; presence of snow in northern regions during winter.

Infrastructure

Modern Scandinavian architecture; wooden houses in rural areas; urban centers with contemporary buildings and historical sites.

Culture

Traditional wooden stave churches, Viking heritage sites, and modern art installations; presence of public art and design elements.

Bonus

Distinctive red and white houses, presence of reindeer in rural areas, and scenic views of fjords and mountains.
