China

Language

Mandarin Chinese (Simplified characters), Cantonese, and other regional dialects

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are typically white with a blue band at the top; they also include a combination of letters and numbers in Latin script.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in Chinese characters, with some bilingual signs in English in urban areas; signs often have red and yellow colors.

Environment

Diverse geography including mountains, plains, deserts, and rivers; urban areas often have tall buildings and dense populations.

Infrastructure

Modern architectural styles in cities, with a mix of traditional Chinese architecture; extensive use of high-rise buildings.

Culture

Presence of Chinese characters on signage, traditional markets, and temples; cultural elements such as red lanterns and pagodas in certain areas.

Bonus

Street View captures often show crowded urban environments, bicycles, and electric scooters; look for distinct red and gold decorations in certain cultural areas.
