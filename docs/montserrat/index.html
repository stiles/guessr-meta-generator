Montserrat

Language

English is the official language, with a standard Latin alphabet and no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left side; they often feature the coat of arms of Montserrat.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are primarily in English, following the British style, with clear typography and often using reflective materials.

Environment

The terrain is characterized by volcanic hills, lush greenery, and a tropical climate; the landscape includes beaches, cliffs, and the Soufri√®re Hills volcano.

Infrastructure

Buildings are typically colonial-style, with some modern structures; urban areas feature a mix of single-story and two-story homes, often with vibrant colors.

Culture

Distinctive markers include Caribbean influences in architecture, music, and festivals, as well as the presence of Irish heritage reflected in place names.

Bonus

Look for the presence of English-style telephone boxes, tropical flora, and occasional views of the volcanic landscape.
