France

Home > Europe > Western Europe > France

Language

French; uses the Latin alphabet with accents (é, è, ê, ë, ç, à, ô, î)

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue strip on the left featuring the EU flag and the 'F' for France.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically blue for motorways, green for non-motorway roads, with white text; many signs include symbols and are often accompanied by pictograms.

Environment

Diverse landscapes including rolling hills, vineyards, coastal areas, and urban settings with a mix of modern and historical elements.

Infrastructure

Distinctive architectural styles such as Haussmannian buildings in cities, medieval castles in rural areas, and modern glass structures in business districts.

Culture

Presence of cafés, boulangeries, street markets, and cultural landmarks like the Eiffel Tower or the Louvre.

Bonus

Street View often features cafés with outdoor seating, distinctive street art, and the presence of bicycles or scooters.
