Language

The official language is Sesotho, with English also widely used. The alphabet is Latin-based with no special characters.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue strip at the top displaying the letters 'LS' for Lesotho.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the left side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are typically in English and Sesotho, with reflective backgrounds. They often feature pictograms.

Environment

Lesotho is characterized by mountainous terrain, with high elevations and a semi-arid climate. The landscape includes rolling hills and valleys.

Infrastructure

Traditional Basotho architecture includes round huts with thatched roofs. Urban areas may feature modern buildings but often retain a rustic charm.

Culture

Look for traditional clothing such as the Basotho blanket and distinctive hats. Culturally significant structures include mosques and churches.

Bonus

You may encounter shepherds with sheep or goats, and the iconic Basotho pony is a notable animal in rural areas.
