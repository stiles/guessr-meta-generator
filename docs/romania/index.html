Romania

Language

Romanian; uses the Latin alphabet with special characters: ă, â, î, ș, ț.

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue band on the left displaying the EU flag and the country code 'RO'.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are generally blue or green for directions, with white text; warning signs are usually triangular.

Environment

Varied landscapes including the Carpathian Mountains, the Danube River, and flat plains; mixed forests and agricultural fields.

Infrastructure

A mix of modern and historical architecture; notable styles include Brutalist, Neo-Romanian, and Byzantine influences.

Culture

Presence of traditional wooden churches, castles (e.g., Bran Castle), and rural villages with distinct folk culture.

Bonus

Prominent use of the Romanian flag colors (blue, yellow, red) in public spaces and buildings; distinctive local advertisements in Romanian.
