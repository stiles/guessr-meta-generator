Marshall Islands

Language

Marshallese; uses the Latin alphabet with some additional letters like 'ṃ', 'ō', and 'ļ'

Cars

Vehicle registration plates are white with a blue border, often with the text 'Republic of the Marshall Islands'.

Driving

Vehicles drive on the right side of the road.

Signs

Road signs are usually in English and Marshallese, with a mix of standard and unique local signage.

Environment

Consists of atolls and islands with sandy beaches, palm trees, and clear blue waters; limited elevation.

Infrastructure

Buildings generally have a tropical style, often one or two stories, with thatched roofs and vibrant colors.

Culture

Traditional canoes, woven palm fronds, and local artwork can be seen; cultural events and festivals are prominent.

Bonus

Presence of tropical vegetation, limited urban areas, and the overall laid-back atmosphere.
